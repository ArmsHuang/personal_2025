Click2 Play

C2P is based on HTML5 architecture design and development of a complete set of advertising technology. It is compatible with player-supported mobile devices (iOS and Android systems) as well as PCs and Macs and is available to all major browsers. For advertisers, they can easily use C2P ads on mainstream social media to publish as C2P media.

In this project, it is a rare experience to design C2p Mascot in the final interesting nature of the game. In the design process, it is also a rare experience for teams to vote. Although I really like every design!
